the browser, reduced

Less browser. More of what you opened it for.

Blanc keeps the everyday browser tools close without building a dashboard around them. Choose the part of your session you want to make quieter.

01

the blanc island

One small control surface. The whole browser.

Tabs, navigation, the current site, and page controls live together, so the page stays in front.

Meet the Island ↗

02

built in

Fewer ads. Less tracking. No extension required.

Blanc includes ad and tracker blocking at the browser level, with a quiet per-page count and an option for sites you choose to allow.

How blocking works ↗

03

a clean trail

Private tabs that stay out of the record.

Private pages do not enter Blanc history, session restore, or reopen-closed. Close the private chip when you are finished.

Read about private tabs ↗

04

keyboard first

One shortcut to move through your whole session.

Command or Control L searches open tabs, favorites, history, and named groups. Type / to run a browser command.

Explore commands ↗

05

a little order

Keep the tabs you need. Tuck away the rest.

Name a group for the task in front of you. Blanc keeps the active one on the Island while the rest stay close, not constantly visible.
