independent by design

A browser with one person accountable for it.

Blanc is built by Anthony J. Loria through Bananify in Rochester, New York. Product decisions, design, development, and support all lead to the same desk.

the maker

A direct line to the person building it.

Anthony designs and develops Blanc, writes its release notes, and answers its support email. Being small means feedback does not travel through departments before it reaches someone who can act on it.

the lineage

New browser. Long-running practice.

Bananify was established in Rochester in 2024. It continues product and design work Anthony began under AJLMEDIA in 2006. Blanc is the studio's browser: focused, practical, and built for the long work of making software better release by release.

the model

Founder-controlled, without an ads business.

Blanc has no venture funding and no investors setting its product agenda or exit timetable. It is not funded by selling ads or turning browsing activity into an audience product. That keeps the incentives simple: make a browser people want to keep using.

supporters

The browser is complete without a purchase.

Every browser feature is free. A one-time $19 Supporter purchase helps fund the work and unlocks three extra app-icon colorways—a cosmetic thank-you, not a subscription or a feature gate.
