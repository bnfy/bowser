a clean trail

Private tabs that stay out of the record.

Pages opened in a private tab are not written to Blanc history and are not restored when you reopen the browser.

what stays private

Use a private tab, then leave no Blanc trail behind.

Not in history.

Pages you visit in a private tab stay out of Blanc's browsing history, so they do not become part of the local record.

Not waiting after a restart.

Private tabs are excluded from session restore and reopen-closed. They do not quietly return the next time you open Blanc.

One simple exit.

The private chip on the Island is a quick way to close the active private tab when you are finished.

good to know

Private does not mean anonymous.

Private tabs keep activity out of Blanc's local history and session restore. They do not create a separate web identity: cookies and site storage are shared with regular tabs, and websites, your network, or an employer may still observe activity.
