good to know

A few straight answers.

Is Blanc free?

Yes. A one-time supporter purchase unlocks extra app-icon colorways, but no browser features are locked behind it.

Does Blanc block ads?

Blanc includes ad and tracker blocking with a per-site exception. No blocker can promise to remove every ad or tracker.

What do private tabs do?

They keep pages out of Blanc history, session restore, and reopen-closed. They are not a separate web identity.

Which systems does Blanc support?

Blanc is available for macOS, Windows, and Linux.
